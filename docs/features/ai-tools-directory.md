# AI Tools Directory Submission Guide

## Overview

The AI Tools Directory is a curated collection of AI tools and services available to the Prompt Manage community. Submitting your tool is completely free and helps you reach thousands of potential users.

## Benefits of Listing Your Tool

- **Free Exposure**: Get your tool in front of thousands of AI enthusiasts
- **SEO Benefits**: Backlink from a high-authority domain
- **Community Trust**: Verified badge for quality tools
- **Direct Traffic**: Users can click through to your website
- **No Cost**: Completely free to submit and list

## Submission Process

### 1. Navigate to the Submission Page

Visit [/directory/submit](https://promptmanage.com/directory/submit) or click the "Submit a Tool" button on the [AI Tools Directory](https://promptmanage.com/directory) page.

### 2. Sign In

You'll need to be signed in to submit a tool. If you don't have an account:

- Click "Sign In" in the top navigation
- Create a free account with your email
- Verify your email address

### 3. Fill Out the Submission Form

The form is organized into several sections:

#### Basic Information

- **Tool Name** (required): The official name of your AI tool
- **Website URL** (required): Your tool's main website
- **Short Description** (required, max 300 characters): A concise description that appears in directory listings
- **Full Description** (optional): Detailed information about your tool's capabilities and features

#### Company Information

- **Company Name**: The company behind the tool
- **Company Website**: Your company's main website
- **Company Logo URL**: Direct link to your logo image (recommended: 200x200px, PNG or SVG)
- **Banner Image URL**: Hero image for your tool page (recommended: 1200x400px)
- **Contact Email** (required): For verification and updates
- **Founded Year**: When your company was established

#### Categorization

- **Primary Category** (required): Select the main category that best describes your tool
- **Secondary Category**: Optional additional category
- **Key Features** (required): Comma-separated list of main features
- **Use Cases** (required): Comma-separated list of how people use your tool

#### Pricing Information

- **Pricing Model** (required): Choose from:
  - Free: Completely free to use
  - Freemium: Free tier with paid upgrades
  - Paid: Requires payment
  - Free Trial: Trial period before payment
- **Pricing Tier**: Starter, Professional, Enterprise, etc.
- **Monthly Price**: Price in USD for monthly subscription
- **Annual Price**: Price in USD for annual subscription
- **Pricing Details URL**: Link to your pricing page
- **Free Trial**: Check if you offer a free trial
- **Trial Duration**: Number of days for the trial

#### Technical Details

- **Platforms** (required): Select all that apply (Web, iOS, Android, Windows, Mac, Linux, API)
- **AI Models Used**: Comma-separated list (e.g., "GPT-4, Claude, Gemini")
- **Integrations**: Tools and services you integrate with
- **API Available**: Check if you provide an API
- **Open Source**: Check if your tool is open source
- **GitHub URL**: Link to your GitHub repository (if open source)

### 4. Submit for Review

After filling out the form:

1. Review all information for accuracy
2. Click "Submit Tool"
3. You'll receive a confirmation message
4. Your submission will be reviewed by our team

## Review Process

### Timeline

- **Initial Review**: 1-3 business days
- **Verification**: We may contact you via the provided email
- **Approval**: Tools are typically approved within 5 business days

### Approval Criteria

Your tool will be approved if it:

- ✅ Is a legitimate AI tool or service
- ✅ Has a working website
- ✅ Provides accurate information
- ✅ Offers value to the AI community
- ✅ Complies with our [Terms of Service](https://promptmanage.com/terms)

### Rejection Reasons

Tools may be rejected if they:

- ❌ Contain false or misleading information
- ❌ Are not actually AI-related
- ❌ Have broken or non-functional websites
- ❌ Violate intellectual property rights
- ❌ Promote illegal activities

## After Approval

### Your Tool Page

Once approved, your tool will have a dedicated page at `/directory/your-tool-slug` featuring:

- Tool name, logo, and banner image
- Full description and key features
- Pricing information
- Technical details and integrations
- Company information
- "Visit Website" button linking to your site
- Social sharing options
- Favorite/bookmark functionality

### Directory Listing

Your tool will appear in:

- Main directory grid
- Category-specific listings
- Search results
- "Most Popular" and "Highest Rated" sorts (based on user engagement)

### Verification Badge

High-quality tools may receive a "Verified" badge, which:

- Appears on your tool card and detail page
- Indicates our team has verified your tool's quality
- Increases user trust and click-through rates

## Best Practices

### Optimize Your Listing

**1. Write a Compelling Description**

- Focus on benefits, not just features
- Use clear, jargon-free language
- Highlight what makes your tool unique
- Keep it concise but informative

**2. Choose the Right Category**

- Select the most relevant primary category
- Add a secondary category if applicable
- This helps users find your tool

**3. Provide High-Quality Images**

- Logo: Clear, recognizable, professional
- Banner: Eye-catching, shows your tool in action
- Use consistent branding

**4. List Key Features**

- Focus on 5-8 main features
- Use clear, benefit-oriented language
- Avoid marketing fluff

**5. Define Clear Use Cases**

- List 3-5 specific use cases
- Help users understand when to use your tool
- Be specific and practical

### SEO Tips

- Use relevant keywords naturally in your description
- Include your tool name and category
- Provide complete information (helps with search rankings)
- Keep your listing updated

### Engagement Tips

- Encourage users to favorite your tool
- Share your directory listing on social media
- Link to your directory page from your website
- Respond to user feedback (when review system launches)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is there a cost to submit my tool?

No, submitting and listing your tool is completely free.

### How long does approval take?

Most tools are reviewed within 1-3 business days and approved within 5 business days.

### Can I submit multiple tools?

Yes, you can submit as many tools as you want, as long as each is a legitimate AI tool.

### Can I update my listing after approval?

Yes, contact support@promptmanage.com with your updates. Self-service editing is coming soon.

### What if my tool is rejected?

We'll email you with the reason. You can address the issues and resubmit.

### Can I remove my listing?

Yes, contact support@promptmanage.com to request removal.

### Do you offer featured placements?

Not currently, but we're exploring premium placement options. Contact us for early access.

### How do I get the "Verified" badge?

Our team awards verified badges to high-quality tools during the review process. Focus on providing complete, accurate information and a great user experience.
